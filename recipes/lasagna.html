Ingredients

  • 1 pound - Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 - Egg (beaten)
  • 15 ounces - Part-Skim Ricotta Cheese
  • ¼ cup - Fresh Parsley (chopped)
  • 2 ½ cups - KRAFT Shredded Low-Moisture Part-Skim Mozzarella Cheese (divided)
  • ½ cup - KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese (divided)
  • 24 ounces - CLASSICO Tomato And Basil Pasta Sauce
  • 1 cup - Water
  • 12 - Lasagna Noodles (uncooked)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Drain the fat.
  3. Add the pasta sauce, water, and 1/4 cup of the Parmesan cheese to the skillet. Stir well.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, parsley, egg, and remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  5. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce onto the bottom of a 13x9-inch baking dish.
  6. Layer 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  7. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  8. Sprinkle 1 cup of the mozzarella cheese over the ricotta mixture.
  9. Repeat the layers twice.
  10. Top with the remaining 4 lasagna noodles.
  11. Spread the remaining meat sauce over the noodles.
  12. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  13. Cover the baking dish with foil.
  14. Bake for 45 minutes.
  15. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  16. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
